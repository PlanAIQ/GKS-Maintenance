About GKS

Owned, Operated &
On the Job

GKS Maintenance was founded by Shawn and Laura Sharrow, who still run the business hands-on today — quoting jobs, checking in on-site, and standing behind every clean.

Behind them is a trained team of cleaning professionals who handle the day-to-day work across Port Huron and St. Clair County, from commercial buildings and Airbnb turnovers to full residential clean-outs and specialized dust extraction.

Whether it's Shawn, Laura, or a member of their crew on site, you get the same standard: show up on time, do the job right, and treat every space like it's our own.
