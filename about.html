About GKS

Family-Owned. Professionally Managed.
Trusted to Deliver.

GKS Maintenance is a locally owned and operated cleaning company founded by Shawn and Laura Sharrow. While many companies step away from the day-to-day operations, Shawn and Laura remain actively involved in every aspect of the business—from meeting clients and providing estimates to visiting job sites and ensuring every project is completed to the highest standard.

Backed by a reliable team of trained cleaning professionals, GKS Maintenance proudly serves businesses and homeowners throughout Port Huron and St. Clair County. Our services include commercial cleaning, vacation rental turnovers, residential clean-outs, post-construction cleaning, and specialized dust extraction.

No matter the size of the job, our commitment remains the same: show up on time, communicate clearly, pay attention to the details, and deliver results you can count on. Whether it's Shawn, Laura, or a member of our experienced team at your property, you'll receive the same level of professionalism, care, and respect every time.

At GKS Maintenance, we don't just clean spaces—we help create healthier, safer, and more welcoming environments for our clients.
